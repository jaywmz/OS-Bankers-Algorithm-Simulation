Log:

Deadlock Detected

A deadlock has been detected in the system. This occurs when the processes are in a state where they are waiting for resources held by each other, causing a circular wait condition.

Why it happened:

  • Processes are requesting more resources than what is available.
  • The current allocation of resources does not allow any process to proceed.

How to prevent:

  • Ensure that the sum of maximum resource requests does not exceed the total resources available.
  • Use resource allocation strategies that avoid circular waits.
  • Review and adjust the maximum resource claims for each process.
